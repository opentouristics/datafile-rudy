Ryszard Haider was a forester of the Bargłówka Forestry, who retired after 50 years of service. 
This place was built in his memory and it's were his farewell party took place. 
This was a special place for Mr. Ryszard and this is why it was chosen to commemorate his person.
Next to the shelter grows the last tree planted by the forester, beech "Ryszard". 
You can also find a stone with an engraved date of the outbreak of the greatest fire in local forests and post-war Poland. 
Curently it is a place where hikers, cyclists and others like to take a rest during their excursion.
