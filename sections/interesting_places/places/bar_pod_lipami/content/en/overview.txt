"Under the Linden trees" bar will suprise you with a unique character of its interiors and a chill atmosphere in the backyard. 
This place is filled with intresting objects collected over the years by the owners. 
Here you can try homemade burgers or sit by the bonfire and indulge yourself with grilled sausages.
The bar is located close to the forest, so inside you can meet people resting after their excursion 
as well as those who are just about to go on the trail. It's also a popular place among locals.
