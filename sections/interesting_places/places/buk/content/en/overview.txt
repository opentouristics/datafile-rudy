Uroczysko Buk is the former favorite vacation spot of the princely family. Finds
here are, among others Victoria Mountain commemorating the daughter of the Queen of England. After II
World War II, a well-known holiday resort with a swimming pool was established here,
hotel, restaurant and concert bowl. The resort fell into decline in the 1990s.

is decaying. A real treat for urbex fans.
