Located in the middle of picturesque Racibórz county forests, the local beach is
a popular place for summer recreation among the inhabitants of Kuźnia Raciborska
and the surrounding villages. In this area you can find beach volleyball court
and a campsite. The beach is sandy and clean, there are designated swimming
areas for children as well as experienced swimmers. You can rent paddleboats
here. The beach also has a playground with slides, swings and a sandpit. There
is a cafe where you can buy ice cream, eat a hot meal or enjoy a cold beer. It
is open during the summer season.
